Science Stream

Subjects include Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics. This stream opens doors to careers in engineering, medicine, research, and emerging technologies like AI and biotechnology.

Commerce Stream

Ideal for students interested in finance, business, and economics. Core subjects include Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, and Mathematics. Careers include Chartered Accountancy, Finance, Business Management, and Entrepreneurship.

Arts Stream

Perfect for those with a flair for creativity and an interest in humanities and social sciences. Subjects include History, Sociology, Psychology, Political Science, and Literature. Careers range from Journalism and Social Work to Teaching, Law, and Design.

Vocational Courses

These courses teach specific skills and are perfect for students keen on entering the workforce early. Examples include Web Designing, Fashion Designing, Computer Hardware, and Beauty & Wellness.
